📦 For Suppliers

List Your Equipment

Turn your idle AV equipment into steady income. Join 100+ suppliers earning on UrbanAV.

1

Register & Complete KYC

Download the UrbanAV Supplier app, create your account, and complete KYC verification (Aadhaar + PAN). This ensures trust and credibility for all buyers.

2

List Your Equipment

Add your AV equipment with photos, specifications, pricing per day, and availability calendar. The more detail you add, the more bookings you'll get.

3

Receive Booking Requests

When buyers request your equipment, you'll get a notification on the app. Review the request, check your availability, and accept or decline. No obligation to accept every request.

4

Deliver & Verify with OTP

Once a booking is confirmed and payment is in escrow, deliver your equipment to the buyer. Share the 6-digit delivery OTP with the buyer. This OTP confirms delivery and releases payment from escrow to your account.

5

Collect Equipment After Use

At the end of the rental period, coordinate with the buyer for pickup. A return OTP confirms the equipment is returned in good condition. Your earnings are transferred to your bank account.

6

Build Your Reputation

Positive reviews mean more bookings. Provide great service, maintain your equipment, and watch your rating grow. Top-rated suppliers get featured on the platform.

7

Get Paid Faster

No more chasing clients for payments. With our escrow system, payment is guaranteed before delivery. Withdrawals are processed within 24-48 hours to your linked bank account.
