🔒 Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you register as a buyer on UrbanAV, we collect your name, phone number, email address, and delivery address. This information is used to facilitate equipment rentals and communicate booking updates. Your KYC documents are verified through government-approved verification services.

We also collect device information, IP addresses, and usage data to improve our platform and prevent fraud.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process and manage equipment rental bookings
  • Verify your identity for secure transactions
  • Send booking confirmations, reminders, and delivery updates via SMS and WhatsApp
  • Facilitate secure escrow payments and refunds
  • Connect you with verified AV equipment suppliers
  • Provide customer support through our helpline

3. Escrow Payment Protection

Your payment is held in a secure escrow account managed by UrbanAV. The payment is only released to the supplier after you verify delivery using the OTP system. This ensures your money is protected until equipment is confirmed delivered.

4. Data Sharing

We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With AV equipment suppliers for order fulfillment (your delivery address and contact)
  • With our payment and escrow service providers for transaction processing
  • With government authorities if required by law
  • With fraud prevention services to protect against unauthorized access

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) to protect your data in transit. Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 encryption. Your payment information is never stored on our servers — all payment processing is handled by Razorpay.

6. Communication Preferences

By registering on UrbanAV, you consent to receiving SMS and WhatsApp notifications for booking updates, delivery OTP codes, and promotional offers. You can opt out of promotional messages by contacting our support team.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at support@urbanav.in for any data-related requests. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to maintain session state and improve user experience. These are essential for the platform to function. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.
