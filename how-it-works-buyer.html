How It Works

Rent professional AV equipment in 4 simple steps. Secure, verified, and hassle-free.

1

Search & Discover Equipment

Browse our marketplace of verified AV equipment. Filter by category (projectors, LED walls, speakers, lighting), location, and event type. View photos, specs, pricing, and supplier ratings all in one place.

2

Request a Quote & Book

Found what you need? Send a booking request with your event dates and requirements. The supplier confirms availability and pricing. Once agreed, pay securely through our platform.

3

Payment Held in Escrow

Your payment is collected and held in our secure escrow system. It is not released to the supplier until equipment is successfully delivered and verified. This protects both parties.

4

Delivery with OTP Verification

Supplier delivers and sets up your equipment on the agreed date. Upon delivery, you receive a 6-digit OTP via SMS. Share this OTP with the supplier to confirm receipt. This triggers payment release from escrow.

5

Use Your Equipment

Enjoy your event with professional AV setup. Our platform tracks your booking period and sends reminders before the return date.

6

Return & Final Verification

At the end of your rental period, coordinate with the supplier for pickup. A second OTP confirms the return. Once verified, your deposit (if any) is returned, and you can leave a review for the supplier.

🔒 Escrow Payment Protection

Your money is never sent directly to the supplier. It stays in our secure escrow until:
✅ Equipment is delivered and verified via OTP
✅ You confirm everything is as described
✅ The rental period is complete

⚡ Same Day Delivery

Need equipment urgently? Many of our suppliers offer same-day delivery in major cities. Just check the availability calendar on the listing and book before 12 PM.

🚫 Free Cancellation

Changed your plans? Cancel before the supplier dispatches and get a full refund. No questions asked, no hidden charges.
