Terms of Service

Last updated: June 2026

1. Acceptance of Terms

By registering as a supplier on UrbanAV, you agree to these Terms of Service. These terms govern your use of our platform to list and rent out AV equipment to buyers. If you do not agree, please do not use the platform.

2. Supplier Eligibility & KYC

You must be at least 18 years old and possess a valid PAN card and bank account to register as a supplier. You must complete KYC verification (Aadhaar + PAN) before your listings go live. UrbanAV reserves the right to reject suppliers who fail verification.

3. Equipment Listings

You are responsible for ensuring all equipment listings are accurate, complete, and up-to-date. Listing information must include:

  • Clear, high-quality photos of the actual equipment
  • Accurate specifications and condition details
  • Transparent pricing per day/week
  • Availability calendar that reflects real availability

False or misleading listings will be removed, and repeated violations may result in account suspension.

4. Booking Acceptance

You are not obligated to accept every booking request. However, if you accept a booking, you agree to deliver the equipment as described, on the agreed date, and in working condition. Declining accepted bookings may result in penalties.

5. Delivery & OTP Verification

You must deliver equipment on the agreed date and time. At the time of delivery, generate a 6-digit OTP from the app and share it with the buyer only after they physically verify the equipment. OTP confirmation releases payment from escrow to your account.

6. Equipment Condition

All equipment must be in working, clean condition when delivered. You must perform regular maintenance and ensure equipment meets safety standards. Any equipment that poses a safety risk must be removed from the platform immediately.

7. Payment & Commission

Payments are processed through UrbanAV's escrow system. Buyer payment is released to you within 24-48 hours after OTP delivery verification. UrbanAV charges a 5% commission on each successful transaction. There are no listing fees.

8. Returns & Damage

At the end of the rental period, coordinate equipment pickup with the buyer. A return OTP confirms the equipment is returned. If equipment is returned damaged beyond normal wear, document the damage through the app and contact support for dispute resolution.

9. Cancellations

If a buyer cancels before dispatch, the booking is cancelled with full refund to buyer. If you need to cancel an accepted booking, contact support immediately. Repeated cancellations without valid reason may affect your profile rating and visibility.

10. Ratings & Reviews

Buyers can rate and review suppliers after each rental. High ratings improve your visibility and booking rate. Negative reviews with valid concerns must be addressed. UrbanAV may take action against suppliers with consistently poor ratings.

11. Prohibited Activities

Suppliers must not:

  • List equipment they do not own or have permission to rent
  • Circumvent the escrow payment system by requesting direct payments
  • Provide false KYC information
  • Use the platform for any illegal activities

Violations result in immediate account suspension and potential legal action.

12. Liability

UrbanAV acts as a marketplace. We are not responsible for disputes between suppliers and buyers beyond facilitating escrow payments and OTP verification. Suppliers are responsible for the condition of their equipment and any damage claims.

13. Platform Fees

Listing on UrbanAV is free. A 5% commission is charged on successful transactions only. There are no hidden charges. Commission is automatically deducted when payment is released to your account.
