🔒 Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

As a supplier on UrbanAV, we collect your business name, PAN card, Aadhaar number, bank account details, and equipment inventory information. This data is required for KYC verification, payment processing, and listing management.

2. KYC Verification

Your Aadhaar and PAN details are verified through government-approved verification services. This verification is mandatory for all suppliers. Verified suppliers receive a trust badge on their profile, increasing booking rates.

3. Equipment Listings

The photos, descriptions, and specifications you provide for equipment listings are stored on our servers. This content remains yours, but you grant UrbanAV a license to display it on our platform and in promotional materials.

4. Payment Information

Your bank account details are securely stored for processing earnings transfers. We use Razorpay for payment processing and do not store your full bank account number on our servers.

5. How We Use Supplier Data

Supplier information is used to:

  • Verify identity and build trust on the platform
  • Process rental bookings and escrow payments
  • Send booking notifications and delivery reminders via SMS and WhatsApp
  • Calculate and transfer earnings to your bank account
  • Display your profile and equipment listings to buyers

6. Data Sharing

We share supplier information with:

  • Buyers — your name, profile rating, and contact for order coordination
  • Payment providers — for earnings transfers to your bank account
  • Government authorities — if required by law for tax compliance
  • Fraud prevention services — to protect the platform from abuse

7. Escrow Payments

Buyer payments are held in our escrow account. Payment is released to your account within 24-48 hours after delivery is verified via OTP. UrbanAV deducts a 5% commission on successful transactions.

8. Data Security

All supplier data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption at rest and SSL/TLS in transit. KYC documents are stored in secure, access-controlled environments. Only authorized personnel can access sensitive supplier data.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to access and update your profile information at any time. Contact support@urbanav.in to request deletion of your account and associated data. Data retention follows legal requirements.
